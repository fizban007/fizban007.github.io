Musings on Pulsar Magnetospheres

A random collection of projects that are loosely related to
the magnetospheres of pulsars, high energy astrophysics, and physics in general.

Pulsar Magnetosphere Visualization

This is an interactive visualization of the potential structure of the magnetosphere of the pulsar PSR J0030+0451. The visualization accompanies a paper published in the Astrophysical Journal in 2020.

  • Learn more

Quadrupole Explorer

This is an interactive app designed to explore the vacuum magnetic field configuration of a dipole + quadrupole. This app was used to facilitate the PSR J0030+0451 paper mentioned above.

  • Learn more

Rotating Vector Model

This is a visualization of the rotating vector model (RVM) often used to explain the polarization swing observed in the radio emission of pulsars.
